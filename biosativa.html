BIOSATIVA

BIOSATIVA® is completely organic created in most parts from hemp oil and yeast. BIOSATIVA® offers exceptional cleaning power that will emulsify strong pollutants to the extent that they are biodegraded, and this reduces the impact on the environment when cleaning. Normally only highly polluting cleaning agents can achieve the same results.

This product is well suited for many cleaning tasks, specifically stains which are of a fat or oil nature. BIOSATIVA® will help remove stubborn stains such as red wine on carpets or table clothes and coffee stains. It’s very effective for cleaning cooking equipment such as stove tops, ovens and microwave ovens.

This is truly steam cleaning in a bottle. It's safe enough to use in your kitchen and tough enough to remove oil residue from animals unfortunate enough to be caught in an oil spill.

For those who love being outdoors and understand how important it is to be kind to nature, BIOSATIVA® should be your preferred cleaning solution. Why not make this part of your daily cleaning routine? A dilution ratio of 1:10, 250ml will make 2.5L which should last you approximately 8 weeks when batches of 250ml are made.

Dilution ratio recommendations:
  • Stove tops and microwave ovens 1:10.
  • Window cleaning with light soiling 1:50.
