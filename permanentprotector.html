PERMANENT PROTECTOR

Permanent Protector is the ultimate Si02 nano scale coating and was developed to protect surfaces in the extreme environments of the marine, aerospace and military sectors. This coating is extremely resistant to corrosion, abrasion and extreme temperature changes.

Permanent Protector can be used on a wide range of materials, ferrous and non-ferrous materials, plastics, anodized aluminium, powder coated surfaces, glass and many more.

BENEFITS

General Benefits:
  • Ultra-high performance hardness, 7H curing at ambient temperatures and 9H for heat curing.
  • Generally, an undetectable protective coating, it will create a glossy appearance once cured, buffing during application can reduce the glossy look.
  • Creates an easier to clean surface, surfaces remain clean for prolonged periods. Less surface tension, surfaces stay cleaner and look better for longer.
  • Heat resistant up to 700°C working temperatures, cold resistant down to -90°C.
  • Its corrosion resistant to alkali, sea water, salty air and fuels. There is a reduction surface damage that occurs through exposure to the elements.
  • Excellent abrasion resistance.
