SILK QUARTZ

SilkQuartz is an easy two step process - CLEAN & COAT, that simple. Glass surfaces treated with SilkQuartz will repel water, dirt and grimeand will look better for longer and are easier to clean. NO MORE water staining, glass etching or unsightly marks.

SilkQuartz is the advanced way of protecting glass and ceramic surfaces.

GLASS PROTECTION AT ITS BEST!

Where would SilkQuartz be used?
  • Buildings - skylights, shower screens, glass balustrades, ceramic baths, ceramic & glass basins, mirrors, canopies, poolside windors and glass splash backs
  • Auto glass - cars, trucks, buses, trains, RVs, caravans, motorcycles.
  • Marine glass - motorboats, ferries, and yachts
  • Solar panels - building, industrial and marine.
Application - EASY TWO STEP PROCESS - CLEAN & COAT
  • The performance of the coating is determined by the thoroughness of the preparation
  • Determine the state of the surface - in certain instance additional surface preparation is required. See surface prepartion for more detail.

STEP 1 Deep clean with SilkQuartz preparation wipe.

STEP 2 Apply SilkQuartz coating treatment wipe

Once cured lightly buff with a microfiber cloth.

*Detailed instructions are supplied with the SilkQuartz kit.
