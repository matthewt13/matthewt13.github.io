WHO WE ARE

Sursol is based in Auckland, New Zealand and focuses primarily on smart products with a strong leaning towards innovative and environmentally conscious chemical formulations.

We have formed strong relationships with suppliers and customers, locally and abroad. Our supply partners work with specific manufacturers and research companies in Germany. Only the best formulations make it past their stringent testing criteria and become available to customers around the world. Our products undergo continues innovation and testing by our supplier as they respond to the demands of customers across the globe. Once a new product has met the quality criteria, it is then released to the rest of the world unless patents prevent this. We view this concept as a significant advantage as we are accessing the best products available in this field. Our product ethos is performance, value, environment and safety. We are proud that our products have a positive impact on the environment.
